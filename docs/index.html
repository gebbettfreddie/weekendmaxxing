Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026.

Weekendmaxxing is built to respect your privacy. We keep the app simple and we collect as little as possible.

What we collect

  • Nothing that identifies you. The app has no login and no accounts.
  • Saved trips are stored locally on your device only — they never leave your phone.
  • Search inputs (origin, weekend dates, budget) are sent to our flight-data partners purely to return prices. They aren't tied to your identity.

Flight data partners

Flight prices and availability are provided by third-party travel-data APIs. When you choose to book, you're handed off to an airline or partner site to complete the purchase; that site's own privacy policy then applies.

Analytics & tracking

We do not sell your data and we do not use cross-app tracking. Any anonymous, aggregated usage stats are used only to improve the app.
